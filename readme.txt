Beautifulck Widget CK enhance your widgets with a nice design with ease.

== Description ==

<p>Beautifulck Widget CK adds a 'Beautiful Me !' option on your widgets. You can click on it and let the magic do the job.</p>
You can select a theme for your widget title (banner, corner, glossy...), the font color and size and an icon for it.
You can set the background color with gradient, border radius, border color, shadow and more on the widget.
The options are made to let you control the way to show your widget with nice colors and the place of your title.
The admin interface is ajax based and show you a direct preview of your styles selection and how will render your widget.


== Installation ==

1. Unzip the package `beautiful-widget-ck.zip`
2. Upload the folder `beautiful-widget-ck` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
